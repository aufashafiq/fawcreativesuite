Privacy Policy — FAW Creative Suite

Last updated: July 22, 2026

FAW Creative Suite is a personal content management tool. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is used.

Through TikTok's official API and OAuth authorization, this app may access basic profile information (display name, avatar, open ID) and the public video list and metadata from the developer's own connected TikTok account.

This app does not access private messages, payment information, or any data belonging to TikTok accounts other than the developer's own authenticated account.

Data retrieved is used only for personal content organization, archiving, and reference. It is not used for advertising, resold, or shared with any third party.

As the account owner, you can revoke this app's access to your TikTok account at any time through TikTok's account settings.
