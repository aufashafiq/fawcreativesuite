Terms of Service — FAW Creative Suite

Last updated: July 22, 2026

FAW Creative Suite is a personal content management tool used by the developer to organize, archive, and reference their own social media content, including data retrieved from their own connected TikTok account.

This app is intended for personal use by its developer only. It reads publicly available video and profile data from the developer's own authenticated TikTok account, and does not post, modify, or delete any content on TikTok on behalf of any user.

Data retrieved through TikTok's API is used solely for personal content organization and is not sold, shared, or distributed to third parties.

Access to TikTok data is granted only through TikTok's official OAuth authorization flow and can be revoked at any time through TikTok account settings.
